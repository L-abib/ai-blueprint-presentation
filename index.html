How It Works: The RAG AI Engine

To ensure our AI provides accurate, up-to-date answers, we use a powerful technique called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). This two-part process involves first teaching the AI from an expert knowledge base, and then using that knowledge to answer user questions.

Part 1: The Art of Chunking

Before the AI can learn, we must break down large documents into small, meaningful "chunks." This is the most critical step for ensuring accuracy. Here’s a visual explanation:
